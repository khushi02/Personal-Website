Story

My interest in computer science began in high school, where I spent every available weekend coding (and eating free food) at hackathons. I love working with new technologies, so my projects range from facial recognition enabled security systems to augmented reality applications. I gained many hard and soft skills through these hackathons, but there was one thing that stuck with me the most: a passion for learning.

I continue to learn through a variety of internships and personal projects. Currently, I am a blockchain development intern at Building Blocks Technologies, where I have expanded my traditional knowledge through blockchain industry research, smart contract deployment, and decentralized application development. Throughout my career, I plan to continue exploring all aspects of the technology industry.
